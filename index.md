# Privacy Policy for Troll Strike

**Effective date:** July 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Troll Strike ("the App," "we," "us") handles information when you play. Troll Strike is a single-player game and does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

## Information We Collect

**Locally on your device.** The App saves your progress — things like levels completed, deaths, hearts, and your selected character — directly on your device. This information is not sent to us or stored on any server we operate.

**Through advertising.** The App uses Google AdMob to show optional rewarded video ads (for example, to refill hearts). To do this, AdMob may automatically collect and process:
- Your advertising ID (a resettable identifier provided by your device, not your name or account)
- General device information (device type, operating system version)
- Approximate location (typically country or region level, used to determine which ads and privacy rules apply)
- IP address
- Ad interaction data (such as whether an ad was shown, watched, or closed)

We do not control what Google AdMob collects or how it processes this data beyond what's described here. For full details, see [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) and [How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

## How We Use Information

- To show you ads within the App, including personalized ads where permitted
- To measure ad performance and prevent fraud/abuse
- To keep your local game progress saved on your own device

We do not sell your information, and we do not use it for any purpose beyond operating and displaying ads within the App.

## Advertising Consent (EEA, UK, and Switzerland)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, the App will show a consent message the first time you open it, asking whether you agree to personalized advertising. You can change this choice at any time from within the App's settings ("Manage Ad Privacy" or similar option). Outside of these regions, this consent step does not apply, consistent with Google's advertising policies.

## Children's Privacy

Troll Strike is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us using the details below so we can address it.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party service:
- **Google AdMob** — for displaying advertisements. See Google's [Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) for how Google handles this data.

## Data Retention

Your locally saved progress remains on your device until you clear the App's data or uninstall it. We do not retain a separate copy of this information.

## Your Choices

- You can reset or limit ad personalization through your device's ad settings (on Android: **Settings → Privacy → Ads**).
- You can uninstall the App at any time to remove all locally stored data.
- If you're in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, you can change your ad consent choice at any time from within the App.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example to reflect changes in the App or in applicable law. Any changes will be posted at this same URL with an updated effective date.
